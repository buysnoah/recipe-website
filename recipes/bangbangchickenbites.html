bang bang chicken bites

description

spicy, crispy chicken nuggets air fried or baked then tossed in a quick chilli sauce

ingredients

  • frozen crispy chicken nuggets (24 ounces / ~680 grams)
  • mayonnaise (1/2 cup)
  • sweet chili sauce (3 tablespoons)
  • sriracha hot sauce (1 or more tablespoons)
  • sliced green onions (1/4 cup)

steps

  1. preheat air fryer to 300째F / 193째C or oven to 400째F / 205째C

  2. air fryer

    add nuggets in a single layer to air fryer basket and fry until hot and crisp, about 10 minutes. shake half way through cooking. you may need to work in batches.

    oven

    place nuggets in a single layer on baking sheet, then bake in preheated oven until golden, about 12 minutes

  3. meanwhile, stir mayonnaise, sweet chili, and hot sauce together in a large bowl until well mixed.
  4. once nuggets are done, toss hot nuggets in sauce until well coated.
  5. top with chopped green onions before serving.
